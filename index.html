Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device

We built vidnessCam with privacy by default. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

vidnessCam ("the App") is developed by Eren Ayas. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. The App is designed to operate primarily on-device — your video recordings are stored locally on your iPhone and are never uploaded to any server by the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Video or audio recordings — all clips are saved locally to your device storage only
  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs (beyond what third-party SDKs may collect — see below)
  • Crash logs or analytics data from our own servers

Camera & Microphone Access

The App requires camera access to record video. Microphone access is requested to include audio in recordings. All recordings are saved exclusively to your device's local storage and are never transmitted to external servers by the App.

Location (GPS) Access

If you enable the GPS overlay feature (Pro tier), the App requests "When In Use" location access to display your current speed and coordinates on the video frame. Location data is embedded in video clips stored locally on your device and is not sent to any server.

Advertising (Free Tier)

The free version of the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Upgrading to Pro removes all ads.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's purchase and privacy policies apply.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete recordings at any time through the App's gallery or by uninstalling the App. Uninstalling the App removes all associated local data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
