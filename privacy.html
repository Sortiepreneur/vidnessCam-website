vidnessCam is fully offline. No accounts, no cloud, no analytics. Nothing you record is ever transmitted to any server.

Overview

vidnessCam ("the App") is developed by Mehmet Eren Ayas. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. The App is designed to operate entirely on-device — your video recordings are stored locally on your iPhone and are never uploaded to any server by the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Video or audio recordings — all clips are saved locally to your device only
  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising data
  • Crash logs or analytics from our own servers
  • Location data beyond what is embedded locally in your clips (Pro only)

Camera & Microphone Access

The App requires camera access to record video. Microphone access is requested to include audio in recordings. All recordings are saved exclusively to your device's local storage and are never transmitted externally by the App.

Location (GPS) Access

If you enable the GPS overlay feature (Pro tier), the App requests "When In Use" location access to display your current speed and coordinates on the video frame. Location data is embedded only in clips stored locally on your device — it is not sent to any server, and the App does not retain a log of location history.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit 2. We do not receive, see, or store your payment information at any point. Apple's purchase and privacy policies govern the transaction.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control. You can delete individual recordings through the App's gallery at any time. Uninstalling the App removes all associated local data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
